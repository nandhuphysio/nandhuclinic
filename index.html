What is Physiotherapy?

Physiotherapy is a health care profession focused on helping people restore movement and function when affected by injury, illness, or disability. Using techniques like manual therapy, exercises, and education, physiotherapists work to reduce pain, improve mobility, and enhance quality of life.

At Nandhu Physiotherapy Clinic, we tailor our treatments to your individual needs, whether you are recovering from surgery, managing chronic pain, or working to prevent future injuries. Our goal is to help you get back to your everyday activities with confidence and comfort.
